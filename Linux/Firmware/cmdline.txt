### Insert below cmdlines into `cmdline.txt` file, before `quiet splash` parameter ###

# `usbhid.mousepoll=0` : Disables polling for USB HID mouses (reduces lag for wireless mouses)
# `cfg80211.ieee80211_regdom=KR` : Set Wi-Fi regulatory country to South Korea (change `KR` with your country code)

usbhid.mousepoll=0 cfg80211.ieee80211_regdom=KR
